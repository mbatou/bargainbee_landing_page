Download and Install BargainBee

  1. Download the extension: Download BargainBee
  2. Open your browser's extension page:
    • For Chrome: Go to chrome://extensions/
    • For Firefox: Go to about:addons
    • For Edge: Go to edge://extensions/
  3. Enable "Developer mode" (if required)
  4. Drag and drop the downloaded BargainBee file into the extensions page
  5. Confirm the installation when prompted
  6. Pin the BargainBee extension to your browser's toolbar for easy access

Ready to Start Saving?

Click the BargainBee icon in your browser toolbar to log in and start saving money on your online purchases!
